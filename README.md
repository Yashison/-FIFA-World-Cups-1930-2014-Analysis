# ⚽ Football Match Analytics on FIFA World Cups (1930–2014)
Analyzed historical FIFA World Cup data to identify trends in team performance, championship outcomes, and tour nament statistics. • Developed interactive Power BI dashboards to visualize host country performance, home advantage impact, and key tournament metrics.

An interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes historical FIFA World Cup data from **1930 to 2014**, providing insights into team performance, player statistics, goal-scoring trends, and tournament outcomes. The dashboard leverages Power BI's visualization capabilities, DAX calculations, and Power Query transformations to deliver an engaging and data-driven analytical experience.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Football Match Analytics on FIFA World Cups (1930–2014)** project is designed to explore the rich history of FIFA World Cup tournaments through interactive dashboards. It enables users to analyze championship records, team and player performances, match outcomes, and goal-scoring statistics using dynamic filters and visual storytelling.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze FIFA World Cup tournament history (1930–2014).
- Compare team performances across different World Cups.
- Identify the most successful teams and top goal scorers.
- Analyze player contributions and scoring efficiency.
- Evaluate home advantage by comparing home wins, away wins, and draws.
- Build an interactive dashboard for data-driven decision making.

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Overview Dashboard

Provides a summary of FIFA World Cup history with key performance indicators and tournament statistics.

#### Key KPIs
- Total Teams
- Total Matches
- Total World Cups

#### Visualizations
- Most Winning Teams
- Top Goal Scorers
- Home Wins vs Away Wins vs Draws
- Goals by Team

---

### 2. Player Statistics

Analyzes player performance across FIFA World Cups.

#### Key KPIs
- Career Span
- Total Matches
- Total Goals
- Team Contribution %

#### Visualizations
- Goals (1st Half vs 2nd Half)
- Total Goals by Player
- Scoring Efficiency Gauge

#### Filters

- Player Name

### 3. Team Analysis

Provides detailed analysis of team performance.
